Frustration

‹›

Honestly, nothing frustrated me more during this entire semester then trying to put that picture of myself on the 'About Me' section of the home page. It literally took forever. I kept getting a little blue question mark popping up instead of the picture. Once I finally figured that out, the picture was way too big, it was bigger than my entire computer screen. There was a lot of resizing of the picture and posting it on different websites so I could embed it into the website so it actually fit on the screen. Finally, once I got it to work, I realized that the picture quality dropped. Honestly, after all the trouble I went to do get it there, I am completely fine with it. If this was a professional website or if I was actually using it once this class is over, I would find another picture, but for this purpose I think it is fine. I learned from this that not everything is perfect, and nothing is going to work on the first try, but eventually in the end you will (hopefully) get some positive results.

Shannon Butler
